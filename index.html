About UTA

Professional Recruitment Partner for Overseas Employment

UTA Manpower Service is a licensed recruitment company under the Sri Lankan Bureau of Foreign Employment (License No: 2179). We are proud winners of the SLBFE Five Star Award 2022 and Excellence Awards. Our commitment is to provide ethical recruitment and manpower solutions to the Middle East, Europe, and Far East.
